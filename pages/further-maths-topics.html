A-Level Further Mathematics

Core Pure 1 & 2 and Further Statistics 1

These booklets contain compiled exam questions from the Edexcel Further Mathematics specification, strictly sorted by topic (Matrices, Complex Numbers, Vectors, etc).

    Key points:

  • Please check the box upon completing a topic. I recommend using one device and a non-private browsing mode to ensure your progress is saved.
  • Arranged by difficulty: Questions start with routine calculations and progress to complex, synoptic modeling problems.
  • Aim to complete every question. The harder questions often combine multiple topics (e.g., using Matrices to solve Differential Equations).
