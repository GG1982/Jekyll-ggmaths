How this works

These booklets contain every exam question from the Edexcel papers sorted by grade.

    Key points:

  • You MUST achieve 100% on all grade 4 exams. It is the minimum for a pass.
  • You MUST answer grade 4 & 5 exams to pass. The grade 4/5 section of Foundation is the same as Higher.
  • Complete ALL exams before moving to the next grade.
  • Aim for 90%+ on each exam below your target grade. For example, if your target is grade 7. You should achieve 90%+ on each exam for grade 6.
  • If you score below 90%, repeat the exam by trying a mock. These are the same questions but with different numbers.
  • For year 9 students, complete at least one exam per week.
  • For year 10 students, complete at least two exams per week.
  • For year 11 students, complete at least three exams per week.
