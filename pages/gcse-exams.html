How this works

These booklets contain every exam question from the Edexcel papers sorted by grade.

    Key points:

  • You MUST achieve 100% on all grade 4 booklets. It is the minimum for a pass.
  • Begin at grade 4 and finish all booklets. Do not be complacent. Many students have dropped 'easy' marks because they thought they knew better.
  • Aim for 90%+ on each booklet below your target grade. For example, if your target is grade 7. You should achieve 90%+ on each booklet for grades 4, 5 & 6.
  • If you score below 90%, repeat the booklet by trying a mock. These are the same questions but with different numbers.
  • Complete all booklets before moving to the next grade.
  • For year 9 students, complete at least one booklet per week.
  • For year 10 students, complete at least two booklets per week.
  • For year 11 students, complete at least three booklets per week.
