GCSE Topics

How this works

These booklets contain every exam question from the IGCSE Edexcel papers sorted by topic.

    Key points:

  • Please check the box upon completing a topic. I recommend using a non-private browsing mode to ensure changes are saved.
  • IGCSE Maths is the international version of GCSE Maths. Both are by Edexcel, and the questions are very similar.
  • I have arranged the questions in order of difficulty for you.
  • Grade 4-6 topics will still contain grade 7+ questions towards the end, so you should attempt them.
  • Aim to complete at least the first ten questions per booklet.
