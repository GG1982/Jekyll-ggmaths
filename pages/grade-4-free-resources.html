How to get a grade 4!

All grade 4 exam questions with video solutions

What you see here are the first four questions from every single exam paper going back to 2017.

I've put these in orders of papers one, two, and three, and then arranged these into booklets of about eight questions each.

What this means for you is that if you can score 100% on these booklets, you can be confident of achieving a grade 4.
