MAT 2007 - 2023

How this works

In this section, you will find all the MAT past papers from 2007 to now, along with the official mark schemes from Oxford. There were some small changes in 2018, but the main content has stayed the same since 2007.

    Key points:

  • The pass mark is usually 80+ marks per paper!
  • Question 1 has ten multiple-choice parts, each worth 4 marks. Show any rough work in the answer booklet and record answers on the grid.
  • Questions 2 to 6 should be answered in the designated spaces in the answer booklet, with each worth 15 marks. You can continue your answers on the blank pages if needed.
