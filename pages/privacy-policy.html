We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your email address from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

10. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data (your email address).
  • Right to rectify inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erase your personal data (unsubscribe).
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time (unsubscribe).
  • Right to complain to the ICO (Information Commissioner's Office).

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link provided in every newsletter email.

To exercise any other rights, please contact us at info@ggmaths.co.uk.
